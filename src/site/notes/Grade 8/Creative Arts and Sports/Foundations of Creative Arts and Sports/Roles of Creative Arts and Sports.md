C/Arts and Sports has social roles and economic roles.

# Social roles of C/Arts and Sports

**Question: What are the social roles of C/Arts and Sports?**

1. **It preserves culture** - through C/Arts and Sports, communities can pass down their traditions and culture through generations.
2. **Fosters social cohesion** - C/Arts and Sports brings us together from different backgrounds to enjoy together.
3. **Promotes identity** - C/Arts and Sports helps in expressing an individuals identity. It shows ones personality and values.
4. **Promotes good health** - C/Arts and Sports, especially in sports and dance, can keep people fit. C/Arts and Sports is also theraputic and relaxing in categories like Art.
5. **A source of entertainment** - C/Arts and Sports brings enjoyment and fun by watching performances and games, and even looking at artistic pieces.
6. **Promotes patriotism** - C/Arts and Sports allows for expressions of national anthems, patriotic songs, national symbols and national olympic events.

# Economic roles of C/Arts and Sports

**Question: What are the economic roles of C/Arts and Sports?**

1. **It creates employment opportunities** - there is a wide range of careers that come from C/Arts and Sports like dancing, coaching, sports players, artists and others.
2. **It attracts tourists** - Other people from the world come to see the performances of C/Arts and Sports. In exchange, we earn money.
3. **It gives government revenue** - the government can tax people earning money from C/Arts and Sports and earn profit.
4. **Poverty eradication** - C/Arts and Sports can give opportunities to the less fortunate to earn income. In turn, poverty is reduced.

# Making a storyboard about the roles of C/Arts and Sports in the society

**Question: What is a storyboard?**

- A storyboard is a series of images, sketches or drawings arranged in an order to tell out a story.

The steps involved in making a storyboard are:

- Collecting the materials needed.
- Preparing the background
- Collecting and cutting pictures
- Sticking the images
- Displaying the images.

## How to prepare the background of a storyboard

**Question: How can we prepare the background of a storyboard?**

- There are 2 techniques we can use.
    - Wash technique
    - Splatter technique.

### Wash technique

**Question: What is wash technique and what does it involve?**

- Wash technique involves using less paint and a lot of water to make a background.
- The paint has to be diluted to get a light like background.

### Splatter technique

**Question: What is splatter technique and what does it involve?**

- Splatter technique involves flicking the paintbrush with paint to make random patterns.

## Materials needed in making a storyboard

**Question: What are the materials needed in making a storyboard?**

- Images and drawings of the roles of C/arts and sports.
- Scissors.
- Adhesives.
- A surface.
- For the background:
    - Paint
    - Paintbrushes
    - Water.
